About me:

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio page. I'm Eric West, born in Chicago in the late 1980's, and an avid amatuer. I have recently begun learning to code, as an avenue to advance my existing career in tech. My interests include music, armorsmithing, sewing, design, fabrication, computer gaming, Dungeons & Dragons, Star Wars, and costuming. I've been a member of a 501c3 chartiy costuming club since 2007, The Mandalorian Mercs Costuming Club, and have been a member of it's official podcast since 2008.
